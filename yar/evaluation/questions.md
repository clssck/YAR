# Evaluation Questions

Questions for benchmarking YAR RAG quality across the ingested document corpus.
Each question is designed to test a specific retrieval/reasoning capability.

## Medical Domain

1. What are the main symptoms of COVID-19 and how long after exposure do they typically appear?
2. What complications can COVID-19 cause in the cardiovascular system?
3. How is COVID-19 transmitted between people?
4. What is the difference between Type 1 and Type 2 diabetes?
5. What are the long-term complications associated with diabetes?

## Finance Domain

6. What is a stock exchange and what purpose does it serve?
7. How does the NASDAQ differ from the New York Stock Exchange in terms of trading mechanism?
8. What is cryptocurrency and how does blockchain technology enable it?
9. What was the total market capitalization of all publicly traded stocks worldwide by end of 2023?
10. What role do market makers play in stock exchanges?

## Climate Domain

11. What are the primary human activities driving climate change?
12. How has carbon dioxide concentration changed since the pre-industrial era?
13. What are the main renewable energy sources that can replace fossil fuels?
14. What environmental impacts has climate change caused in the Arctic?
15. What is the Paris Agreement and what temperature target did nations agree to?

## Sports Domain

16. Who founded the International Olympic Committee and when were the first modern Olympics held?
17. How have the Olympic Games evolved since their ancient origins in Greece?
18. What caused the cancellation or postponement of Olympic Games throughout history?
19. What is the FIFA World Cup and how often is it held?
20. What were the ancient Olympic Games and where were they held?

## Cross-Domain / Multi-Hop

21. How did the COVID-19 pandemic affect the Olympic Games?
22. What role do international organizations like the WHO play in addressing both health crises and climate change?
23. How has climate change been linked to the emergence of diseases like COVID-19?
24. What economic impacts do major global events (pandemics, Olympics) have on stock markets?
25. What preventive measures are recommended for both COVID-19 and climate change?
